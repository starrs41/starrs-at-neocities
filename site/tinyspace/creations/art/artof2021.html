Art of 2021

I experimented with a lot of different art programs this year. IIRC they were Paint Tool Sai 2, CSP, Blender, Medibang Paint, Colors3D, and Procreate. In the end, I got attached to Procreate (though to be honest, CSP has the most features overall).

I really liked the life-like textures of the Procreate brushes, plus using Procreate meant that I could draw in secret without people sneaking up behind me to check my laptop screen ^_^ So, as you can see, I went all out ahaha.
