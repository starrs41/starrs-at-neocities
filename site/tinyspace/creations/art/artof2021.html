Art of 2021

I experimented with a lot of different art programs this year. IIRC they were Paint Tool Sai 2, CSP, Blender, Medibang Paint, Colors3D, and Procreate. In the end, I got attached to Procreate ^o^

I really liked the life-like textures of the Procreate brushes, plus using Procreate meant that I could draw privately without people sneaking up behind me to check my laptop screen and bombard me with questions (this happens a lot) :"]
