Miscellaneous

Here are some links that contain different types of media, some of which differ from the rest.

pixabay
a site with free photos, illustrations, videos, music, and more. an account is not needed to download anything.
Graphics
pexels
a site filled with free high-quality pictures! they're free to download, even without an account!
unsplash
another site with free high-quality pictures. you don't need an account to download most of the pictures, though for the rest, an account and subscription is needed.
stockvault
contains a wide variety of graphics that are free to download without an account.
irasutoya
the site of the illustrator, Takashi Mifune, who is famous for creating the illustrations commonly seen around Japan. the illustrations are very cute, and free to use!
